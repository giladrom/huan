Welcome to Huan!

Huan connects you to other pet owners, creating a worldwide community that protects pets - and helps make sure your best friends will always stay by your side.

How does this all work?

Huan Tags

Your Huan Tag is not like other pet tags - It's actually a tiny computer, sending signals which can be received by a smartphone.

Your Huan app listens to tags around it, so you'll always know when your pet is near.

What if my pet goes missing?

Missing Pets

When your missing pet is detected by another Huan user, you are immediately notified with the exact location of your pets' whereabouts, while the other Huan user receives your contact information and can call or message you directly.

Huan Tags are detected automatically - even if your phone is in your bag, purse or pocket!

Allow Location Access

Selecting 'Always' makes sure Huan tags will be detected even if the application is running in the background, or if the screen is locked.

Alternatively, go to Settings > Huan > Location and set to 'Always'.

Enable Notifications

Allow notifications in order to be alerted when your missing pet is found, or when a lost pet is detected in your vincinity.

Alternatively, go to Settings > Huan > Notifications and enable 'Allow Notifications'.
